Charades

I like playing Charades because you work in teams and have to act until your team guesses what you're trying to be.

I especially like when we get to make our own Charades words, because then we can make really funny ones like "a dancing monkey".

What You Need

Here are all the tools you will need to play this game

  • Paper
  • Pen
  • Timer

How To Play The Game

Setting Up The Game

  1. On multiple small pieces of paper, write out a word or phrase. One idea is to stick to a theme of "Movies", "Tv Shows" and "Books".
  2. Fold each paper in half to hide what is written on the paper, and put them inside a hat or a bowl
  3. Split your group into two teams

Playing The Game: Do the following steps per team until all pieces of paper have been used

  1. Select a team player who will do the acting for that round
  2. That team player will randomly pick a piece of paper from the hat/bowl
  3. When the team member has had time to read the paper, and signalled that they are ready, the opposing team will begin the timer
  4. The team member with the paper will now have to act out whatever is on the paper, without saying any words verbally
  5. While the team member acts, the rest of the team must guess what is being acted before time runs out
  6. If the team is able to guess the word before time runs out, they get a point
  7. The winner is the team with the most number of points at the end of the game
